Other ways to help..

Even if you aren't able to donate there are other ways to help. One way is simply just sharing about our goals and having other people do the same to raise awareness. Another way you can help is if you have a minor condition or injury, go to your doctor or an urgent care first because alot of the times they can help you, or inform you to go to the E.R, which decreasing waiting times in the E.R for people who need to be seen.
